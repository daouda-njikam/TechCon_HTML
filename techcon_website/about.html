History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local gathering of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most anticipated international technology conferences.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, empower tech professionals, and create opportunities for collaboration across the global tech community.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential minds in technology:

  • Ada Lovelace: Recognized as the first computer programmer. Her visionary ideas paved the way for computing as we know it.

  • Alan Turing: A pioneer in artificial intelligence and cryptography, Turing's work laid the foundation for modern computing.
